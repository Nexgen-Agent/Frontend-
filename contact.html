Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard website redesign typically takes 4-8 weeks, while a comprehensive branding project can take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing for one-time services and monthly subscription plans for ongoing marketing support. Project pricing ranges from $5,000 to $50,000+ depending on scope, while subscriptions start at $499/month.

Do you work with businesses outside the US?

Yes, we work with clients globally. Our team is distributed across multiple time zones, allowing us to accommodate clients in North America, Europe, and Asia. We're experienced in navigating different markets and cultural contexts.

What makes ShootingStar different from other agencies?

We're not just a service provider - we're strategic partners in your growth. Our hybrid approach combines AI-powered systems with human creativity to deliver results that are both data-driven and emotionally compelling. We focus on building sustainable systems, not just executing campaigns.

Can I see examples of your work?

Absolutely! Check out our portfolio page to see case studies and examples of our work across different industries. We're proud of the results we've delivered for our clients and happy to share more details.
